About Us

Our Story

Codacs was founded in 2016 to bridge the knowledge gap between colleges and industry. Founded by Ashish Rakwal, Codacs boasts of world-class teaching faculty and a state-of-art learning platform for Coding education. teaches 14+ Programming courses in Foundation,l Advanced, Data & Development courses such as Machine Learning, Data Science, Web Development, Android and more

Our Vision

To create an alternate platform for students who wish to continue and complete courses by attending live online courses, using a team of ridiculously committed educators who will stop at nothing to impart education, helped by a 24 x 7 support system. By deploying our world class team of industry experts, we aim to educate our learners with the skills they need to advance their professional life to the next level.

Achievements

Codacs ecosystem comprises of 18,000+ students and alumni, 500+ Campus Ambassadors, 1300+ Teaching Assistants, and 250+ employees. Our 4000+ candidates selected in Google, Facebook, Amazon, Flipkart, Microsoft, IBM, HCL and more.
